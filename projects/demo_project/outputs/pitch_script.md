# Pitch Script for Kids Photo Book

## Introduction
Hello everyone, and thank you for joining us today. We are excited to present to you our latest innovation, "아이 포토북 (Kids Photo Book)"—a mobile application designed to help parents easily capture and share the precious moments of their children's growth.

## Product Overview
"아이 포토북" is more than just a photo gallery; it is a storytelling platform that records each special moment with photos, stories, locations, and even the weather of the day. Our mission is to enhance the experience of preserving and sharing cherished memories.

## Project Goals
Our primary goal is to assist parents in conveniently maintaining a growth record for their children. We want to offer an experience that goes beyond simple photo storage by providing features that enrich storytelling and allow memories to be shared in both digital and physical formats.

## Key Features
1. **Album Creation:** Users can select multiple images and compile them into a single themed album.
2. **Detailed Record:** Each moment can be richly detailed with date, location, weather, and comprehensive stories.
3. **Individual Caption:** Add unique captions for each photo to describe the moment more personally.
4. **Date-based Organization:** Albums are grouped and displayed by month for easy browsing.
5. **PDF Export:** Convert your digital albums into printable PDFs, perfect for sharing or preserving in a photobook.
6. **Fullscreen Photo View:** Enjoy photos in full screen for a more immersive experience.

## Technical Stack
We used **React Native** and **TypeScript** for cross-platform development, ensuring both productivity and quality.
The application's navigation is managed through **React Navigation v7**, while **AsyncStorage** facilitates local data management. **Expo-image-picker** and **expo-location** provide image selection and GPS functionalities respectively.

## Future Plans
Looking forward, we plan to integrate cloud sync capabilities and add features like customizable themes and video recording. By continuously enhancing our platform, we aim to create a comprehensive solution tailored to modern parental needs.

## Conclusion
In conclusion, "아이 포토북" offers a powerful yet intuitive way for parents to document their children's lives dynamically and memorably. We are eager to move forward with the development and believe in the great potential of this project to succeed in the market. Thank you for considering our presentation—we welcome any questions you might have.
